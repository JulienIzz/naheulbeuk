Le Donjon de Naheulbeuk – Privacy Policy

(Last Updated: March 2026)

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Le Donjon de Naheulbeuk app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Le Donjon de Naheulbeuk application (the “App”).

Creator and Developer

This application was created and developed by Julien IZZILLO. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at julien@izzillo.fr.

2. Data We Collect

Le Donjon de Naheulbeuk does not collect any personally identifiable data. No information such as your name, email address, or unique device identifiers is collected or processed. The app simply streams audio content and stores your usage preferences locally on your device (playback progress, favorites).

How We Use Your Data

Data stored locally on your device is used solely to improve your user experience, such as remembering your listening progress and preferences. No data is shared with third parties for tracking or advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data remains on your device, we do not maintain any databases storing your personal information. You may delete this data at any time by clearing the application data or uninstalling the App.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to help protect your data. Any communication with our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS). We take precautions to keep your data secure on your device; however, no security system is completely impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Le Donjon de Naheulbeuk does not knowingly collect any personal information from individuals under the age of 13 (or a minor in your jurisdiction) without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so that we can promptly remove the information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. When we do, we will post the updated policy within the App and update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this document. Your continued use of the App following any changes indicates your acknowledgment of the modifications.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at julien@izzillo.fr.
